My Design Process

Initally I meet with my client and get a clear understanding of their needs. Next, I research the topic and find the most relevant data then make a collection to inspire my design. After sketching ideas and mapping out the designs to see what works together, I mockup three of the strongest design ideas. In a client meeting I present the three designs and let them choose which works best. After all the client concerns are addressed, I make adjustments and send the final design for confirmation. Finally I make any revisions necessary and deliever the design in the desired format.
